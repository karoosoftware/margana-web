Margana: The Tactical Word Game

Margana is a daily tactical word puzzle where players build five horizontal words to complete a grid, then tactically release letters to form a final anagram for bonus points

Additional points are earned by finding extra words in all directions, including vertical and diagonal, as well as using words that are palindromes and semordnilaps to gain double points

Players compete on daily and weekly leaderboards, with optional challenges like the daily anagram and Margana madness offering additional scoring opportunities
